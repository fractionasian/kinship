# Kinship — Vietnamese & Chinese Family Terms

Visual reference for Vietnamese and Chinese (Mandarin) kinship terminology. Each position in the family tree shows the correct term for that relationship.

**Live:** [fractionasian.github.io/kinship](https://fractionasian.github.io/kinship)

## Sources

- Luong, Hy Van (1984). "Brother and Uncle." *American Anthropologist* 86(2), pp. 290-315.
- Huang, S. & Jia, W. "Cultural Connotations and Communicative Functions of Chinese Kinship Terms." *American Communication Journal* Vol. 3(3).
- Michigan State University — *Basic Vietnamese* open textbook, Unit 6 Culture Notes.
- Omniglot — Family words in Vietnamese; Family words in Mandarin.
